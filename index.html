Publications

B. Xu, X. Guo, F. Cai, X. Li, M. Yao, Z. Ren · Aerospace Science and Technology

An optimization framework that couples attention-mechanism surrogate models with reinforcement learning to drive source-term-based aerothermal design, improving both efficiency and accuracy over conventional CFD loops.

Physics-Enforced Neural Ordinary Differential Equation for Chemical Kinetics Optimization in Reaction–Diffusion Systems 2026
F. Cai, H. Zhou, Z. Ren · arXiv:2603.28478

A neural-ODE framework that embeds physical conservation constraints into the learned dynamics, enabling robust optimization of chemical-kinetics parameters in reaction–diffusion systems.

F. Cai, H. Zhou, M. Yao, Z. Ren · Applied Thermal Engineering

Applies a deep active-subspace method to quantify and optimize turbulence-model uncertainties in RANS simulations of rounded laidback film-cooling holes, sharpening predictive accuracy.

Efficient Sensitivity Analysis and Optimization of Sectional Soot Model in Burner-Stabilized Stagnation Flame Using Fixed Flux Approximation 2026
F. Cai, X. Su, M. J. Cleary, Z. Ren, A. R. Masri · Combustion and Flame

Introduces a fixed diffusion-flux approximation that yields a ~3× speedup in sensitivity calculations for sectional soot models and reduces the maximum optimization loss by over 50%.

Machine Learning-Aided Exploration of Crossflow Effect on Cooling Effectiveness for Laidback Fan-Shaped Holes 2026
X. Li, F. Cai, B. Xu, M. Yao · Applied Thermal Engineering

Combines machine-learning surrogates with active-subspace analysis to reveal how internal crossflow reshapes film-cooling effectiveness in laidback fan-shaped holes and identify the dominant flow parameters.

Exploiting Active Subspace for Modeling Uncertainty and Optimization in Fan-Shaped Film Cooling Simulation 2026

Uses the active-subspace method to quantify uncertainty from turbulent Prandtl number and SST closure coefficients, then optimizes them to cut average relative errors to roughly 1–4% across blowing ratios.

A deep-learning active-subspace approach that pinpoints dominant parameters and optimizes fan-shaped film-cooling effectiveness, offering an efficient alternative to high-fidelity CFD.

Active Subspace-Aided Global Sensitivity Analysis and Optimization of Turbulence Model Coefficients for Film Cooling Simulations 2025
F. Cai, H. Zhou, M. Yao, Z. Ren · Applied Thermal Engineering 270

Performs active-subspace-based global sensitivity analysis and optimizes turbulence-model coefficients to improve film-cooling predictions across operating conditions.

A Data-Driven Method to Optimize Soot Kinetics Based on Uncertainty Quantification and the Active Subspace Approach 2025
