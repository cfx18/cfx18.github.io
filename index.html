About

I am a researcher working at the intersection of computational fluid dynamics (CFD), uncertainty quantification, and machine learning. My work focuses on data-driven surrogate modeling, active-subspace methods for high-dimensional design exploration, and efficient algorithms for conjugate heat transfer and turbulence modeling — with applications in film cooling, aerothermal design optimization, and chemical kinetics.

Research Interests

  • Uncertainty Quantification
  • Active Subspace Methods
  • Deep Learning Surrogate Modeling
  • Film Cooling & Conjugate Heat Transfer
  • Turbulence Modeling
  • Chemical Kinetics Optimization
